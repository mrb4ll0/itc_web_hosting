About IT Connect

IT Connect was born from a simple observation: talented students were struggling to find quality industrial training placements, while companies were having difficulty discovering promising young talent.

Founded in 2024, our platform has grown to become Nigeria's leading industrial training connection service, serving thousands of students and hundreds of companies across various industries.

We believe that practical experience is essential for shaping the next generation of professionals, and we're committed to making that experience accessible to all.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academia and industry by providing a dynamic platform where ambitious learners gain practical experience and companies discover emerging talent, fostering the next generation of Nigerian professionals.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted industrial training platform, recognized for transforming how students transition from academic learning to professional careers and how companies identify and nurture future talent.

Our Values

Accessibility: We believe every student deserves access to quality industrial training opportunities, regardless of background or connections.

Quality: We maintain high standards by verifying companies and opportunities to ensure valuable experiences for students.

Partnership: We foster collaborative relationships between students, companies, and educational institutions for mutual benefit.

Growth: We're committed to the continuous improvement of our platform and the professional growth of our users.

Innovation: We continuously evolve our platform to meet the changing needs of students and companies in the digital age.

Community: We build supportive networks where students and professionals can connect, share experiences, and learn from each other.

Our Impact

Since our launch, IT Connect has connected over 5,000 students with industrial training opportunities, partnered with 250+ companies across various industries, and facilitated 1,200+ successful placements across 15 states in Nigeria.

Thank you for being part of our journey to transform industrial training in Nigeria.
